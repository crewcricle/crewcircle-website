## LinkedIn Post (Launch Announcement)

We've updated the CrewCircle website to make it easier to find the right tool for your business.

CrewCircle builds practical AI for Australian small businesses. Two flagship products are live now:

TaxFlowAI is a research assistant for Australian tax professionals. Ask ATO questions in plain English, get cited answers from actual public rulings and legislation, upload ATO letters for draft responses, and build a private knowledge base from your firm's own precedents.

LocalMate is local business automation for Aussie SMBs. It handles review replies, local SEO tracking, competitor monitoring, lapsed customer follow-ups, and menu syncing. One dashboard, five jobs done.

Four more tools are in beta: SmartGL for AI bookkeeping, CrewRoster for shift scheduling and timesheets, CardSnap for scanning business cards, and AuRate for ATO rate lookups and ABN validation.

Everything's built for Australian businesses. Australian tax rules, Australian platforms, Australian data.

Have a look at crewcircle.com.au
