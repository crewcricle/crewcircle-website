# Social Media Plan for crew circle

## Overview
This plan outlines the social media strategy for crew circle, a practical AI consultancy focused on Australian businesses. The goal is to establish a strong social presence that showcases AI expertise while building trust with the target audience.

## Target Audience
- Australian small and medium business owners
- Professionals in need of practical AI solutions
- Decision makers looking for no-nonsense technology guidance
- Local community interested in AI adoption

## Platform Strategy

### Primary Platforms
1. **LinkedIn**
   - Focus: Professional expertise, thought leadership, B2B connections
   - Content: Case studies, industry insights, thought leadership articles
   - Posting frequency: 3-4 times per week

2. **Twitter/X**
   - Focus: Real-time updates, quick tips, engagement with trending topics
   - Content: AI news, product updates, community engagement
   - Posting frequency: 5-7 times per day

3. **YouTube**
   - Focus: Educational content, tutorials, interview series
   - Content: How-to guides, tech deep-dives, customer success stories
   - Posting frequency: 1-2 times per week

### Secondary Platforms
4. **Instagram**
   - Focus: Visual content, behind-the-scenes, community building
   - Content: Team photos, office culture, product visualizations
   - Posting frequency: 3-4 times per week

## Content Pillars

### 1. Practical AI Solutions
- Show how AI solves real business problems
- Before/after case studies
- Tips and tricks for AI implementation

### 2. Local Australian Focus
- Australian AI success stories
- Local community events and contributions
- Understanding Australian business landscape

### 3. Technical Expertise
- Deep dives into AI concepts

- Industry trends and insights

### 4. Company Culture & Values
- Behind-the-scenes content
- Team introductions
- Company milestones and achievements
