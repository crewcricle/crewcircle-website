- **Account Handle**: @mybyconsultancy
- **Account URL**: https://www.linkedin.com/company/mybyconsultancy
- **Setup Priority**: High - Primary professional network for B2B connections

**Setup Steps**:
1. Go to linkedin.com
2. Click "Sign up" or "Create account"
